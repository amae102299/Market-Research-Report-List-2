<p><p>Golf mats can be classified into two main types: combined mats and one-piece mats. Combined mats are designed with multiple layers, typically consisting of a rubber base layer and an artificial turf top layer. These mats provide a realistic feel and improved shock absorption. On the other hand, one-piece mats are made from a single material and do not have distinct layers. They are often more durable and easier to clean, making them a popular choice among golfers. Both types serve the purpose of allowing golfers to practice their swings and shots indoors or on driving ranges.</p></p>
