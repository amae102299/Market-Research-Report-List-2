<p><ul><li>Single Phase</li><li>Three Phase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Auto Transfer Switch PDU market offers different types of switches: single phase and three phase. Single phase switches are designed to handle power distribution for residential and small commercial applications that use a single alternating current waveform. They are capable of transferring power supply automatically. On the other hand, three phase switches are intended for industrial and larger commercial applications that require higher power capacity. They can efficiently distribute power across three alternating current waveforms, ensuring seamless power transfer in case of a power outage.</p></p>
