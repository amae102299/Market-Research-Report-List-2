<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Wireless Power Receivers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inductive Coupling Wireless Power Receivers</li><li>Capacitive Coupling Wireless Power Receivers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wireless power receivers refer to devices that receive power wirelessly from a power transmitter. Inductive coupling wireless power receivers use electromagnetic fields to transfer power between the transmitter and receiver. They work by using coils to create a magnetic field, which induces a current in a nearby receiver coil. On the other hand, capacitive coupling wireless power receivers use electric fields to transmit power. They work by using capacitors to store and release energy in the form of electric fields, which then powers the receiver.</p></p>
