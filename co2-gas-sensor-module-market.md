<p><p>The CO2 Gas Sensor Module market is classified into different types based on the technology used. NDIR (Non-Dispersive Infrared) CO2 Sensors measure CO2 levels by analyzing the light absorption characteristics of the gas. EC (Electrochemical) CO2 Sensors utilize a chemical reaction to detect CO2 levels. PAS (Photoacoustic Spectroscopy) CO2 Sensors detect CO2 by measuring the sound waves created by gas absorption of light. The "Others" category includes any CO2 sensors that do not fall into the above-mentioned types. These sensors play a crucial role in monitoring and controlling CO2 levels in various industries and applications.</p></p>
