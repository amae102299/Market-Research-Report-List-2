<p><p>RF SAW filters are used in various applications for signal processing. Transversal filters are a type of RF SAW filter that utilize multiple delay lines to provide high selectivity and low insertion loss. They are commonly used in communication systems where precise filtering is required. On the other hand, resonator filters are RF SAW filters that utilize resonating structures to achieve desired filtering characteristics. These filters are used in applications where high performance in terms of selectivity and bandwidth is necessary. Both types of filters play a crucial role in improving signal quality and minimizing interference.</p></p>
