Air insulated load break switches use air as the insulation medium, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Oil-immersed load break switches use mineral oil for insulation, delivering excellent performance in harsh environments. These switches are essential for controlling the flow of electrical current and ensuring safety in various applications.</p></p>
