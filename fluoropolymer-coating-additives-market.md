<p><p>The Fluoropolymer Coating Additives Market consists of various types such as Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE), Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene (FEP), and Polyvinylidene Fluoride (PVDF). Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) is a synthetic fluoropolymer commonly known for its non-stick properties, high heat resistance, and low friction. Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene (FEP) is another fluoropolymer that offers excellent chemical resistance, thermal stability, and electrical insulation properties. Polyvinylidene Fluoride (PVDF) is a durable fluoropolymer with exceptional resistance to UV radiation, chemicals, and extreme temperatures. These additives are widely used in industrial coatings, electrical wires, cookware, and aerospace applications.</p></p>
