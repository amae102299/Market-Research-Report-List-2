<p><p>There are three main types of electric two-wheeler battery technologies in the market: lead-acid battery, lithium battery, and other emerging options. Lead-acid batteries are the most commonly used and have been in use for a long time. They are cost-effective but have limited energy storage and shorter lifespan. Lithium batteries, on the other hand, offer higher energy density, longer lifespan, and quicker charging capabilities but come at a higher cost. Other emerging battery options include nickel-cadmium and nickel-metal hydride, but these are less prevalent in the electric two-wheeler market.</p></p>
