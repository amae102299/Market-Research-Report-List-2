<p><p>Polyester film capacitors are a type of capacitors used in various electronic applications. There are two main types: plain polyester film capacitors and metallised polyester film capacitors. Plain polyester film capacitors are made by sandwiching a plain polyester film between two metal electrodes. On the other hand, metallised polyester film capacitors have a layer of metal deposited on the polyester film, replacing the need for individual electrodes. These capacitors offer advantages such as high reliability, good insulation properties, and low cost, making them popular in the electronics market.</p></p>
