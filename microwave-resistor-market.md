<p><p>The microwave resistor market consists of two main types: thick film sheet and thin film sheet. Thick film sheet resistors are created by depositing a thick layer of resistive material onto a substrate, which offers high power handling capabilities and lower cost. On the other hand, thin film sheet resistors are made by depositing a thin layer of resistive material, providing better accuracy and stability but at a higher cost. Both types cater to the growing demand for microwave resistors in various applications.</p></p>
