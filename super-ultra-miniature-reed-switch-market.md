<p><p>The Super Ultra-miniature Reed Switch market consists of various types such as Form A, Form B, Form C, and Others. Form A refers to a normally open switch that becomes closed when a magnet is present. Form B is a normally closed switch that opens when a magnet is near. Form C, also called changeover or transfer type, contains both normally open and normally closed contacts, which change state in the presence of a magnet. Additionally, the market includes other unique switch types not covered by these three forms.</p></p>
