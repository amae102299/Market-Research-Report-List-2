<p><p>The spray sunscreen market is divided into three main types: physical sunscreen, chemical sunscreen, and physical and chemical sunscreen. Physical sunscreen contains minerals like titanium dioxide or zinc oxide that form a protective barrier on the skin to reflect UV rays. Chemical sunscreen absorbs UV rays through active ingredients like avobenzone or oxybenzone. Physical and chemical sunscreen combines the benefits of both types, using minerals and chemicals to provide broad-spectrum protection against UVA and UVB rays. Each type offers unique advantages and caters to different preferences and skin types.</p></p>
