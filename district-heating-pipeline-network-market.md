<p><p>The district heating pipeline network market offers two main types of pipelines: pre-insulated steel and polymer. Pre-insulated steel pipes consist of a steel core surrounded by insulation material, providing excellent thermal efficiency and durability. These pipes are ideal for long-distance or high-temperature applications. On the other hand, polymer pipelines are made of high-density polyethylene (HDPE) or polypropylene (PP) materials, providing flexibility and ease of installation. These pipes are suitable for shorter distance heating distribution systems. Both types play a crucial role in efficiently and effectively distributing heat in district heating networks.</p></p>
