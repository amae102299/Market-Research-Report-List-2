<p><p>Aerospace and automotive MRAM (Magnetoresistive Random Access Memory) market consists of two main types: Toggle MRAM and STT-MRAM. Toggle MRAM utilizes a toggle-switching mechanism based on magnetic tunnel junctions (MTJs) to store data. It offers high speed, endurance, and non-volatility, making it suitable for aerospace and automotive applications that require fast and reliable memory. On the other hand, STT-MRAM (Spin-Transfer Torque MRAM) uses spin-polarized current to switch the magnetic moments, offering improved data storage density, lower power consumption, and compatibility with existing CMOS technology. Both types provide advantageous memory solutions for aerospace and automotive sectors.</p></p>
