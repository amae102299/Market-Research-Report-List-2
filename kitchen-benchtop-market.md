<p><strong>The Kitchen Benchtop Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Press Molding</li><li>Casting Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Kitchen benchtop market types include two popular methods of manufacturing: press molding and casting molding. Press molding involves pressing a mixture of materials such as crushed stone or quartz with resin into a mold, resulting in a solid and durable benchtop surface. On the other hand, casting molding involves pouring a liquid resin mixture into a mold, allowing it to harden and form the desired shape. Both methods produce high-quality benchtops, but press molding tends to be more cost-effective, while casting molding allows for more intricate designs and customization options.</p></p>
