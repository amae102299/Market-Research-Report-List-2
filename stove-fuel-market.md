<p><p>Stove fuel, also known as cooking fuel, is a type of fuel used to power stoves and cook food. LPG is a cleaner and more convenient fuel option compared to traditional fuels. Biogas is produced through the decomposition of organic matter and can be used directly in stoves or converted into LPG.
