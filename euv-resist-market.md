<p><p>The EUV resist market can be categorized into two types: positive resist and negative resist. Positive resist refers to a type of resist material that becomes more soluble upon exposure to EUV (extreme ultraviolet) light, resulting in increased solubility in the developed areas. Conversely, negative resist is a resist material that becomes less soluble upon EUV exposure, leading to reduced solubility in the developed areas. These different types of resists enable the creation of intricate patterns during the lithography process, contributing to the production of advanced microchips and electronic devices.</p></p>
