<p><p>The photonic crystals market can be categorized into three types: three-dimensional photonic crystals, two-dimensional photonic crystals, and one-dimensional photonic crystals. Three-dimensional photonic crystals are structures that possess a periodic variation of refractive index in all three dimensions. Two-dimensional photonic crystals have a periodic variation of refractive index in two dimensions, forming a surface with unique optical properties. Lastly, one-dimensional photonic crystals possess a periodic variation of refractive index in only one dimension, offering particular optical characteristics. These different types cater to varying applications and contribute to the overall growth of the photonic crystals market.</p></p>
