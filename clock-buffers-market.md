<p><p>Clock buffers are electronic components used to divide or distribute clock signals in electronic devices. Clock buffer market types include differential, single-ended, universal, and others. Differential clock buffers are designed to transmit signals using differential pairs, providing effective noise reduction and high-speed performance. Single-ended clock buffers operate with a single voltage level and are less complex, suitable for lower-speed applications. Universal clock buffers offer flexible configurations to support both differential and single-ended signals. The "others" category represents clock buffers with unique features or functionalities, catering to specific requirements or niche applications.</p></p>
