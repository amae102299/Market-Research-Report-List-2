<p><p>The GPS positioner market includes two major types: independent positioner and advanced positioner. Independent positioners are stand-alone devices that allow users to determine their precise location by receiving signals from GPS satellites. They are mainly used for basic navigation purposes in personal applications. On the other hand, advanced positioners are more sophisticated and offer additional features like real-time tracking, geofencing, and data logging. These positioners are commonly utilized in industries such as transportation, logistics, and fleet management, where accurate and continuous location data is crucial for operational efficiency.</p></p>
