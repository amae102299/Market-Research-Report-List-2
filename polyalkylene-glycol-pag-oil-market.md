The first type is PAG oils that are not soluble in water, meaning they do not mix or dissolve in water. These oils are widely used in industrial applications such as lubricants, hydraulic fluids, and heat transfer fluids. The second type is PAG oils that can be dissolved in water, making them suitable for applications like water-based lubricants, metalworking fluids, and textile auxiliaries. These oil types offer different properties and applications in various industries.</p></p>
