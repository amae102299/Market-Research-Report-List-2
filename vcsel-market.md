<p><p>VCSEL (Vertical Cavity Surface Emitting Laser) refers to a type of laser diode used in various applications. VCSEL Market Types can be categorized into Single Mode and Multimode markets. Single mode VCSELs are designed for precise communication over long distances, achieving a narrow beam width with a single, well-defined light mode. On the other hand, Multimode VCSELs are optimized for short-range data communication and exhibit multiple light modes, enabling high-speed data transmission. Both market types cater to different needs based on the required communication distances and data transmission speeds.</p></p>
