<p><p>The shoe orthotics market is divided into two main types: rigid orthotics and soft orthotics. Rigid orthotics are made of harder materials and provide support for walking or running by stabilizing the foot and correcting any misalignment. They are typically used to treat conditions like flat feet or high arches. On the other hand, soft orthotics are made of softer materials and are designed to cushion the foot and reduce pressure on sensitive areas. They are primarily used to provide comfort and alleviate pain caused by conditions like plantar fasciitis or diabetes.</p></p>
