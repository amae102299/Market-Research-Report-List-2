<p><p>The military tactical communications market consists of several types of communication systems. Manpack radios are compact systems carried by personnel or mounted on vehicles, providing enhanced communication capabilities for specific tasks. Vehicular Intercommunication Radios (VIC) enable seamless communication among crew members inside military vehicles. High Capacity Data Radios (HCDR) transmit heavy data, like images and videos, rapidly. Situational Awareness Video Receivers help troops view live video feeds for improved situational awareness during operations.</p></p>
