<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The digital rail wheel sensor market includes two main types: single wheel sensors and double wheel sensors. Single wheel sensors are designed to detect the presence or absence of a wheel on a railroad track, providing information about train position and speed. On the other hand, double wheel sensors are capable of detecting and measuring the distance between two consecutive wheels, enabling the calculation of a train's axle count and the identification of overloaded axles. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in ensuring the safe and efficient operation of railway systems.</p></p>
