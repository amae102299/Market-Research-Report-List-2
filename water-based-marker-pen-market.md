<p><p>Water-based marker pens are available in three different types: broad tip, fine tip, and brush tip. Fine tip markers have a narrower tip, allowing for precise and detailed work such as outlining or writing. Brush tip markers have a flexible brush-like tip that can create both thin and thick lines, perfect for expressive artwork or calligraphy. Each marker type offers unique benefits and versatility for various artistic or writing purposes.</p></p>
