<p><p>The Isolated Interface ICs market is categorized into three types based on the number of channels: 2 channel interface, 4 channel interface, and 8 channel interface. The 2 channel interface refers to ICs that can connect and isolate two different electronic systems or devices. Similarly, the 4 channel interface allows the isolation and connection of four different systems, while the 8 channel interface provides isolation and connection capabilities for eight systems. These ICs enable the exchange of data and signals while maintaining electrical isolation between the interconnected systems.</p></p>
