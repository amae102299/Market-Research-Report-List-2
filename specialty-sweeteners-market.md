The increasing prevalence of lifestyle-related diseases like obesity and diabetes, coupled with rising consumer awareness about healthy eating habits, has fueled the demand for low-calorie and sugar-free food products. One prominent trend is the rising popularity of plant-based sweeteners. Manufacturers are also focusing on developing innovative sweeteners that provide a similar taste profile to sugar but with lower calorie content.</p><p>Another trend is the incorporation of specialty sweeteners into a wider range of products, including baked goods, dairy products, and beverages. Food manufacturers are recognizing the market potential of offering healthier options to consumers, leading to the introduction of various sugar-free or reduced-sugar products.</p><p>In conclusion, the specialty sweeteners market is witnessing robust growth driven by increasing consumer awareness about health and wellness.
