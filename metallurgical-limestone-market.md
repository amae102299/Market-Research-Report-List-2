<p><p>Metallurgical limestone is a type of limestone used in the production of metals such as steel and iron. There are different types of metallurgical limestone, including dolomitic limestone, calcitic dolomite, and magnesian limestones. Dolomitic limestone contains both calcium carbonate and magnesium carbonate, while calcitic dolomite is primarily composed of calcium carbonate with a smaller amount of magnesium carbonate. Magnesian limestones have a higher magnesium content compared to the other two types. These different types of metallurgical limestone provide varying levels of calcium and magnesium, which are important for the metallurgical processes.</p></p>
