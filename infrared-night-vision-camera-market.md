<p><p>The infrared night vision camera market includes three main types of technology: thermal imaging, near infrared imaging, and low light imaging. Thermal imaging cameras use heat signatures to detect objects and create images with temperature variations. Near infrared imaging cameras utilize near infrared light to enhance visibility in low light conditions. Low light imaging cameras employ sensitive sensors to capture and amplify available light. These technologies are widely used in surveillance, security, and military applications to enhance visibility and detect objects in darkness or low light environments.</p></p>
