<p><p>Insect repellent towelettes are available in two main types: DEET and DEET-Free. DEET-based repellents are effective against a wide range of insects and have been widely used for several decades. They provide long-lasting protection and are especially effective in areas with high mosquito populations. On the other hand, DEET-Free repellents use alternative ingredients like picaridin or essential oils to repel insects. While they may not offer the same level of effectiveness as DEET-based products, they are often preferred by individuals seeking natural or chemical-free options.</p></p>
