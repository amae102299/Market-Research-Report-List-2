<p><strong>The Projection Display Chip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CMOS Chips</li><li>DMD Chips</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Projection display chips are key components used in devices such as projectors and head-up displays. CMOS chips, also known as Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor chips, are widely used in projection displays due to their low power consumption and high integration capabilities. They convert electrical signals into images by manipulating and controlling pixels. DMD (Digital Micromirror Device) chips, on the other hand, use microscopic mirrors to reflect light and create images. These chips offer benefits like high resolution and fast response time, making them suitable for applications requiring high image quality and brightness.</p></p>
