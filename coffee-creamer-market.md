<p><p>Coffee creamer refers to a product used to enrich the taste and texture of coffee. There are two main types: powdered coffee creamer and liquid coffee creamer. Powdered coffee creamer is made by drying a mixture of milk solids, sweeteners, and emulsifiers into a fine powder. It is convenient to use, has a longer shelf life, and comes in various flavors. On the other hand, liquid coffee creamer is a liquid form of creamer that can be poured directly into coffee. It offers a smoother and creamier taste, with options like flavored or non-dairy varieties.</p></p>
