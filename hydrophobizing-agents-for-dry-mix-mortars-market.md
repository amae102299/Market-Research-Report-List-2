<p><p>Hydrophobizing agents for dry-mix mortars are substances that are used to make mortars water-repellent. There are different types of hydrophobizing agents available in the market. Hydrophobic polymers are synthetic compounds that create a water-resistant barrier on the mortar surface. Silicone-based products contain silicones that repel water and protect the mortar from moisture damage. Fatty acid type products use fatty acids to make the mortar hydrophobic. Metallic stearates are compounds derived from metal salts and stearic acid that provide water repellency to the mortar.</p></p>
