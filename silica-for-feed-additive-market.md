<p><p>Silica for feed additive is available in various types such as powder, micro-pearl, and super-fine powder. Powdered silica consists of small particles that can be easily mixed with feed ingredients. Micro-pearl silica has a spherical shape and provides better flowability and dispersion in feed formulations. Super-fine powder silica consists of extremely fine particles, which allows for better absorption and utilization by animals. These different forms of silica offer flexibility and convenience in incorporating silica into animal feed for enhanced nutrition and health benefits.</p></p>
