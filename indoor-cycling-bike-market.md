<p><p>The indoor cycling bike market can be categorized into two main types: magnetic resistance and felt pad resistance. Magnetic resistance bikes use opposing magnets to create resistance, allowing for a smooth and quiet ride. They offer precise resistance levels and are more durable. On the other hand, felt pad resistance bikes use friction between a brake pad and flywheel to create resistance. They tend to be more affordable but are noisier and may require occasional maintenance to replace the brake pads.</p></p>
