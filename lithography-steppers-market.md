<p><p>Lithography steppers are devices used in the semiconductor industry for printing integrated circuits onto silicon wafers. Different market types of lithography steppers include UV (Ultraviolet), DUV (Deep Ultraviolet), EUV (Extreme Ultraviolet), and Nanoimprint. UV steppers use longer wavelengths to expose the circuit patterns while DUV steppers use shorter wavelengths for finer resolution. EUV steppers utilize extremely short wavelengths for even finer details. Nanoimprint involves using physical contact and mold to transfer patterns onto the wafer. These market types offer varying levels of precision and resolution for producing advanced semiconductor chips.</p></p>
