<p><p>Piezo stack actuators are a type of electro-mechanical device that use the piezoelectric effect to produce precise and controlled linear motion. These actuators consist of multiple piezoelectric layers stacked together, with each layer expanding or contracting when an electric voltage is applied. This expansion and contraction motion generates a corresponding linear displacement, allowing for fine and sensitive positioning in a variety of applications.</p><p>The global piezo stack actuators market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
