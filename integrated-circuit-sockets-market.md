<p><p>Integrated Circuit Sockets is a market that encompasses various types of sockets used in the production and testing of integrated circuits. Dual In-Line Memory Module (DIMM) Sockets are specifically designed for memory modules and provide a connection between the module and the system's printed circuit board. Production Sockets are used during the manufacturing process to securely hold the integrated circuit in place and allow for easy replacement. Test/Burn-In Sockets are utilized to test and evaluate the performance of integrated circuits during the burn-in process before final packaging.</p></p>
