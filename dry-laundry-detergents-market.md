<p><p>The dry laundry detergents market can be categorized into various types, including surfactant, bleach, stabilizer, and others. </p><p>Surfactants are the main ingredients that help in reducing the surface tension of water, making it easier for detergent to penetrate fabric fibers and remove dirt and stains. Bleach is another type that helps in whitening and brightening clothes by removing stains and discoloration. Stabilizers are substances added to detergents to enhance their effectiveness and prevent them from deteriorating over time. Other types include enzymes, fragrances, and optical brighteners, which provide additional benefits to the detergent.</p></p>
