<p><p>Nd doped fibers are a type of optical fibers that are doped with the rare earth element neodymium (Nd). These fibers are widely used in fiber lasers and amplifiers due to their high gain and efficiency. There are two main types of Nd doped fibers in the market: single clad fiber and double clad fiber. Single clad fiber has a central core doped with Nd surrounded by a cladding layer, while double clad fiber has an additional pump cladding layer that allows for better pump absorption and higher output power.</p></p>
