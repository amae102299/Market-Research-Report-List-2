<p><p>IQF (Individual Quick Freezing) seafood refers to a freezing technique that enables the preservation of seafood products by freezing them individually to maintain their natural texture, taste, and quality. In this process, each piece of seafood is frozen separately at extremely low temperatures, typically between -30°C and -40°C, using specialized equipment. This freezing technique ensures that the ice crystals formed are small and do not damage the cellular structure, ultimately resulting in high-quality frozen seafood.</p><p>The IQF seafood market has witnessed significant growth in recent years. The increasing demand for convenience food products, coupled with the rising consumer awareness regarding the benefits of frozen seafood, is driving the market growth. IQF seafood offers several advantages, such as longer shelf life, easy storage, reduced food waste, and convenient cooking procedures, which are contributing to its popularity among consumers.</p><p>Additionally, the growing health consciousness among consumers, who are seeking nutritious and low-fat food options, is further boosting the demand for IQF seafood products.
