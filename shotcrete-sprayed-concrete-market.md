<p><p>Shotcrete and sprayed concrete are construction techniques used to apply concrete onto surfaces. In the dry mix method, dry ingredients are mixed with water at the construction site and then sprayed onto the surface using a high-pressure hose. This method allows for precise control and is commonly used for vertical or overhead applications. On the other hand, in the wet mix method, all ingredients are premixed before being pumped through a hose and sprayed onto the surface. This method is faster and more commonly used for large-scale projects. Both techniques offer efficient and durable concrete applications.</p></p>
