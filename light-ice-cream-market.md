<p><p>The light ice cream market consists of two main types: soft ice cream and hard ice cream. Soft ice cream has a smooth and creamy texture, produced by a machine that incorporates air into the mixture. It typically contains a lower fat content and is served at a higher temperature, making it easier to scoop. On the other hand, hard ice cream has a denser texture and is served at a lower temperature. It usually contains a higher fat content and requires more effort to scoop.</p></p>
