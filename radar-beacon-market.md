<p><p>A radar beacon, also known as a transponder beacon or radar responder, is a type of electronic device that is used to enhance the detection and tracking capabilities of radar systems. It is typically equipped on targets or objects that need to be easily identified by radar systems, such as ships, aircraft, and vehicles. The radar beacon emits a signal that is picked up by radar systems, allowing them to accurately locate and track the object.</p><p>The radar beacon market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. A major factor driving this growth is the increasing demand for enhanced surveillance and security systems across various industries. The adoption of radar beacons in military and defense applications for target identification and tracking purposes is also contributing to the market growth.
