<p><p>Fluorine release films refer to protective coatings used in various industrial applications. They are classified based on their thickness into three market types: below 30 um, 30 um-60 um, and above 60 um. Below 30 um films are thin coatings that provide a high level of flexibility and conformability, making them suitable for delicate surfaces. Films ranging from 30 um to 60 um offer a balanced combination of flexibility and strength, making them ideal for general applications. Films above 60 um are thick coatings with excellent durability and resistance, often used for heavy-duty purposes that require added protection.</p></p>
