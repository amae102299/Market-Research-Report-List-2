<p><p>The High Temperature Silicone Single Core Cable market consists of two main types: Solid Single Core Cable and Strand Single Core Cable. Solid Single Core Cable refers to a cable made of a single solid wire, which offers high durability and resistance to high temperatures. On the other hand, Strand Single Core Cable is made of multiple thin strands of wire twisted together, providing increased flexibility and easier installation while maintaining high temperature resistance. Both types of cables cater to the demand for high temperature applications, but differ in their structural composition.</p></p>
