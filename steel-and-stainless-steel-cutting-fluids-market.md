<p><p>The steel and stainless steel cutting fluids market consists of two main types: water-based cutting fluids and cutting oils. Water-based cutting fluids are mixtures of water and other additives that provide cooling and lubrication during the cutting process. They are environmentally friendly and offer good rust protection. On the other hand, cutting oils are petroleum-based fluids that provide excellent lubrication and are commonly used in heavy-duty cutting operations. They offer better performance in terms of tool life and surface finish but are less environmentally friendly compared to water-based cutting fluids.</p></p>
