<p><p>Sealer additives are substances used to improve the performance and durability of sealer coatings. They can be classified into three main types: powder, gelatinous, and others. Powder additives are finely ground substances that are easily mixed with the sealer. Gelatinous additives have a thick and jelly-like consistency, making them easy to apply and control. Other types of additives include liquid or emulsion forms, wax-based additives, or even granular materials. Each type offers specific advantages and may be suitable for different applications or desired properties in the sealer coating.</p></p>
