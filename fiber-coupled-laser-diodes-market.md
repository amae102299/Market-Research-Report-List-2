<p><strong>The Fiber Coupled Laser Diodes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode</li><li>Multimode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fiber Coupled Laser Diodes market is categorized into two types: Single Mode and Multimode. Single Mode diodes have a smaller core size, granting a single, precise beam with little spreading. They offer higher power density and better signal quality, making them ideal for long-distance communication and medical applications. On the other hand, Multimode diodes have a larger core size, resulting in multiple beams that spread out. They are suitable for short-distance data transmission and industrial applications. Both types cater to distinct requirements in various industries.</p></p>
