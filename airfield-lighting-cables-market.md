<p><strong>The Airfield Lighting Cables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Primary circuit</li><li>Secondary circuit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The airfield lighting cables market consists of two types: primary circuit and secondary circuit cables. Primary circuit cables are used to transmit high power electricity from the power source to the airfield lighting fixtures. These cables are designed to handle high voltages and are installed underground. On the other hand, secondary circuit cables are used to connect the lighting fixtures to the primary circuit cables. These cables carry lower voltage signals and are installed above ground to ensure easy maintenance and replacement of lighting fixtures.</p></p>
