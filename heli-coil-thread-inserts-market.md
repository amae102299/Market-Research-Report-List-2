<p><p>Heli-Coil Thread Inserts are available in two market types: Tanged Thread Inserts and Tangless Thread Inserts. Tanged Thread Inserts have tangs or small protrusions that need to be broken off after installation, which can be a disadvantage. On the other hand, Tangless Thread Inserts do not have tangs, making them easier to install and removing the need to break off any tangs. Both market types provide efficient and reliable thread reinforcement solutions for various industrial applications.</p></p>
