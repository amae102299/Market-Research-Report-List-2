<p><p>Crowbar resistors, also known as crowbar overvoltage protectors, are passive electronic components that are used to protect sensitive electronic circuits from overvoltage conditions. This helps prevent damage to the circuit components and ensures the safe operation of the system.</p><p>The current outlook of the crowbar resistors market is highly positive, with steady growth expected in the coming years.
