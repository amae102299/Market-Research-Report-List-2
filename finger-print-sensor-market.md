<p><p>The fingerprint sensor market offers various types of sensors. Capacitive sensors use an array of tiny capacitors to measure the electrical properties of a fingerprint. Ultrasonic sensors emit high-frequency sound waves to capture the unique patterns of a fingerprint. RF sensors use radio frequency signals to analyze the tiny ridge details on a fingerprint. Besides these, there are other sensors available that may utilize different technologies, such as optical or thermal. These sensors play a crucial role in providing secure access control and authentication solutions across various applications including smartphones, laptops, door locks, and payment devices.</p></p>
