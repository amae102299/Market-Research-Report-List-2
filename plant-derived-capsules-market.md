<p><p>The market for plant-derived capsules can be categorized into two main types - HPMC capsules and others. HPMC capsules are made from hydroxypropyl methylcellulose, a plant-based material that is suitable for vegetarian and vegan consumers. These capsules offer a range of advantages such as enhanced stability, improved moisture protection, and resistance to cross-linking. On the other hand, the category of "others" includes plant-derived capsules made from various materials such as pullulan, cellulose, starch, and agar. These capsules provide alternative options for encapsulating pharmaceuticals, dietary supplements, and other products.</p></p>
